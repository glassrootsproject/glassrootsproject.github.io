About Us

Our Model

The goal of our project is to partner with the Heritage Glass Museum to leverage our pride for our community for social, cultural, environmental, and economic change. We want to make Glassboro’s tremendous history and glass collections more accessible to students and the community, revamping the outdated Heritage Glass Museum to bring Rowan students, local businesses, and community members together through our shared history. In order to raise awareness and funds for a full renovation, we would create a documentary highlighting Glassboro’s history and host screenings, organize street fairs and art shows to involve local artists, and plan a large open house event with group tours, glassblowing demonstrations, and the unveiling of the revived museum.
