Meet the Team

The Glass Roots Project Team

We are a dedicated group of students from the Honors Concentration at Rowan University. We met in 2019 as a result of the IDEA challenge

Co-Founder

name: Darien Brown

Year: Freshman

Major: Radio, Television, and Film

Description:

Darien’s passions include film production, writing, and musicals. She plays trumpet in the Honors Jazz Band and is a member of Chi Alpha and Cinema Workshop.

Co-Founder

name: Elizabeth Dunn

Year: Junior

Major: Radio, Television, and Film

Description:

Elizabeth’s passions include film, reading, and classical piano. In 2017, she published a children’s book, MacCarrig, set on the Jersey shore. After a moving tour of the Heritage Glass Museum in Glassboro, she was inspired to continue her exploration of Glassboro and use her love of local history to reach towns across New Jersey and beyond.

Research Executive

name: Lauren Vitale

Year: Sophomore

Description:
